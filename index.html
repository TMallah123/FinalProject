T A L I A ' s F I N A L P R O J E C T

Jewish Food Beyond the Bagel and the Bible!


This website is a culmination of some Jewish food topics that I find most interesting. It is a way for me to combine my love of food, Judaism, and coding. Through engaging with the course content and doing independent research I have created and coded this website.

I have explored topics that I find interesting related to Jewish food and culture. Click on the different options in the top menu to see the blogs I wrote.

Throughout my first semester in college as a vegan I have realized that it not so easy to find good food. I have struggled with not being able to easily cook for myself and get food that I want to eat. I have always loved cooking, baking, and of course eating food. So, not having these things as easily accessible has become a bit of a challenge. Every Tuesday/Thursday this class was my last of the day. We would spend the class talking about food and no matter what it was I left feeling hungry and in the mood for whatever we were talking about. Though talking about the foods that I love makes me sad that I can't eat them or that they are not currently accessible, it also fills me with imense joy. I love learning about the history of the foods that I love, the foods that my grandparents make me, and the food that people of different cultures ate. As I code this website and formulate it's contents I am sitting here with a hungry stomach and an excitement to talk about food.
