Trend 3

Sweet Hummus?

To be honest I really like the different hummus flavors. I like roasted red pepper, beet hummus, everything bagel, caramelized union, and there are many more. But I am cautious to try chocolate flavored hummus. I understand that chocolate and tahini would work well together so I guess adding chickpeas would also work well, but it seems scary. Overall, I do think the flavors would work together, but it feels so American, so not "authentic" and therefore feels wrong.
